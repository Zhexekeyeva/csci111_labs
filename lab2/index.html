General

The third largest city in the country – Shymkent is in the south of Kazakhstan.
This is one of the largest industrial and commercial centers of the Republic, located relatively close to the borders with Uzbekistan and Kyrgyzstan, as well as their capitals - Tashkent and Bishkek.
